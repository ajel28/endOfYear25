About Us

We’re Amir, Erik, and Elijah, three lifelong gamers who came together to build The Vault, a space designed to help players manage and elevate their gaming experience. We know how easy it is to lose track of your growing collection, forget what games you’ve completed, or miss out on new platforms and updates. That’s why we created The Vault: a clean, all-in-one hub where you can track your inventory, stay informed about platforms and trends, and even get help through a built-in AI assistant tailored for gamers.

This project was born out of a shared passion not just for playing games, but for improving the way people interact with them. We've poured our time, creativity, and love for the gaming world into every part of The Vault. Whether you're organizing your library, exploring new titles, or seeking smarter ways to game, we built this platform to be your go-to companion. We're proud of what we've created and excited to continue growing with the gaming community.
